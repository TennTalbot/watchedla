Camera Data Is Incomplete

The camera database does not include every surveillance camera in Los Angeles. New cameras are installed regularly, and not all cameras are mapped in OpenStreetMap or publicly disclosed. Private cameras on private property, temporary law enforcement deployments, and cameras installed after the last data update will not appear in the app.

The absence of a camera marker does not mean an area is free of surveillance.

This App Does Not Make You Safe

Route scores and camera counts are informational only. A route with a lower Privacy Score may still pass cameras not in our database. Using WatchedLA does not protect you from surveillance, harassment, or harm. Do not rely on this app for personal safety decisions.

Privacy Scores are calculated based on the number and type of mapped cameras along a route. They are estimates, not audited measurements. The scoring model may not reflect operational status, camera direction, resolution, or real-time data-sharing agreements between agencies.
